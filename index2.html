Compactors

Salandro's offers Self-Contained and Stationary Compactor Units

Are your current open top containers being picked once a week or more? A compactor may be a better solution to your refuse needs. Compactors on average will hold as much refuse as five open-top containers. Give us a call today to learn how much you could save by switching to a compactor.

Recycling

Salandro's Refuse, Inc. is committed to preserving the environment through our recycling efforts. Salandro's will accept your cardboard, scrap metal, and appliances free of charge. Do the right thing, don't landfill recyclable materials, bring them to us.

Salandro's accepts the following recyclable materials:

  • Old Corrugated Cardboard (OCC)
  • Scrap metal (steel, aluminum, stainless, etc...)
  • Appliances (freon* and non-freon)

*some fees may apply

OCC bales stacked and ready to ship for processing

On average Salandro's recycles over 300 Tons of cardboard per year that would otherwise go to local landfills.
