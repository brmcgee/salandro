Welcome to Salandros

Salandro's Refuse, Inc. located in Crabtree, PA, has been providing quality refuse hauling at reasonable prices for over 40 years. Meeting the needs of our customers from municipalities to large and small contractors to individual homeowners is our top priority. Some things that set us apart from other haulers is our next-day pick-up. Same-day service is also possible in some areas.

Services Connect

In 1979 Salandro's Refuse, Inc. was established in Jeannette Pennsylvania with only a '68 Chevy pickup truck and friendly, reliable garbage service. From there the fleet quickly expanded and Salandro's bought their first rear load garbage truck in 1980, it was a '71 International. Salandro's continued to provide reliable residential curbside pickup and added Roll-off service in 1991. In 1995 residential pick-up was stopped to better focus on roll-off services. Today Salandro's operates a fleet of 6 roll-off trucks out of Crabtree, PA and provides services to Westmoreland and surrounding counties.
